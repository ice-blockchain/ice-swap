## Introduction

The **ION Swap** is designed to provide a user-friendly interface for swapping your ICE v1 tokens into ICE v2 tokens. By leveraging our simple swap solidity smart contract, you can perform token swaps quickly, securely, and with minimal effort.

## Features

- **Simple and Intuitive Interface**: Easy-to-use platform for both beginners and experienced users.
- **Secure Transactions**: All swaps are executed via audited smart contracts on the blockchain.
- **Real-time Swap Progress**: Track each step of your swap process in real-time.
- **Wallet Compatibility**: Supports multiple web3 wallets for your convenience.
- **Testnet Support**: Option to use the platform on the testnet for testing purposes.

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

- **Web3 Wallet**: A compatible cryptocurrency wallet installed (e.g., [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/), Trust Wallet, etc.).
- **ICE Tokens**: Available balance of ICE v1 tokens in your wallet.
- **BNB for Gas Fees**: A small amount of BNB (Binance Coin) to cover transaction fees on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC).
- **Browser Compatibility**: Latest version of Chrome, Firefox, or Edge for optimal performance.

### Accessing the Website

1. Open your preferred web browser.
2. Navigate to the **ION Swap** website: [https://swap.ice.io](https://swap.ice.io)

*Note*: Always ensure you are visiting the correct URL to prevent phishing attempts.

### Connecting Your Wallet

1. **Locate the Connect Button**: In the top-right corner of the website, click on the **"Connect Wallet"** button.
2. **Select Your Wallet**: Choose your preferred wallet provider from the list (e.g., MetaMask, WalletConnect).
3. **Authorize Connection**:
   - A prompt will appear in your wallet application.
   - Confirm the connection to allow the ION Swap website to interact with your wallet.
4. **Network Selection**:
   - Make sure your wallet is connected to the **Binance Smart Chain (BSC)** network.
   - If you're testing, switch to the **BSC Testnet**. [https://swap.testnet.ice.io](https://swap.testnet.ice.io)

*Tip*: If you face issues connecting, refer to your wallet's guide on how to connect to custom networks.

## How to Swap ICE

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. **Enter Swap Amount**:
   - In the **"From"** section, input the amount of **ICE** v1 tokens you wish to swap.
   - The **"To"** section will automatically display the equivalent amount of **ICE** v2 tokens.

2. **Review Swap Details**:
   - Double-check the entered amount.
   - Ensure you have sufficient **ICE** v1 tokens and **BNB** for gas fees.

3. **Initiate the Swap**:
   - Click on the **"Swap"** button to start the swapping process.

4. **Approve ICE Tokens**:
   - A transaction prompt will appear, requesting permission for the swap contract to spend your **ICE** v1 tokens.
   - Confirm the **"Approve"** transaction in your wallet.
   - *Note*: This step allows the contract to access the specified amount of your ICE v1 tokens for swapping purposes.

5. **Confirm Swap Transaction**:
   - After approval, a second transaction prompt will appear to execute the swap.
   - Confirm the **"Swap"** transaction in your wallet.

6. **Wait for Confirmation**:
   - The blockchain will process your transactions.
   - Transactions typically complete within a few minutes, depending on network congestion.

7. **Transaction Completion**:
   - Once the swap is successful, you'll receive a confirmation message.
   - Your **ICE** v2 tokens will appear in your wallet shortly after.

### Swap Progress Tracking

During the swapping process, you can monitor the status of each step:

- **Approving ICE Tokens**:
  - Status indicators:
    - **Pending**: The approval transaction is awaiting confirmation.
    - **In Progress**: The transaction is being processed on the blockchain.
    - **Completed**: Approval is confirmed.

- **Swapping ICE**:
  - Status indicators follow the same format as above.

*Visual Indicators*:
- A checkmark (**✓**) indicates a completed step.
- A loading icon (**⏳**) indicates a step in progress.
- An open circle (**○**) indicates a pending step.

## Transaction Details

- **Transaction Hash**:
  - After each transaction, a unique hash is generated.
  - You can view the transaction details on [BscScan](https://bscscan.com/) by clicking on the provided link.

- **Gas Fees**:
  - Transactions on the Binance Smart Chain require gas fees paid in **BNB**.
  - Fees vary based on network activity.

- **Token Balances**:
  - Post-swap, your **ICE** v1 token balance will decrease, and your **ICE** v2 token balance will increase accordingly.
  - You may need to add **ICE** v2 as a custom token in your wallet to view the balance.
